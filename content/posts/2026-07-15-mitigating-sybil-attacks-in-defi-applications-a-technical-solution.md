# Mitigating Sybil Attacks in DeFi Applications: A Technical Solution

## Introduction

Sybil attacks pose a significant threat to decentralized finance (DeFi) applications, compromising their security and integrity. These attacks involve creating multiple fake identities or accounts to manipulate the system and gain an unfair advantage. In this post, we'll explore a technical solution to mitigate Sybil attacks and enhance the security of DeFi applications.

## The Problem of Sybil Attacks

Sybil attacks are a type of social engineering attack that involves creating multiple fake identities or accounts to manipulate a system. In the context of DeFi applications, Sybil attacks can lead to a range of problems, including:

*   Manipulation of voting systems
*   Manipulation of market prices
*   Elevation of privileges
*   Compromising the integrity of the system
