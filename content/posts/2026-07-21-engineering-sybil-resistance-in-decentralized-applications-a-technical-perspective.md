# Sybil Resistance in Decentralized Applications: A Technical Perspective

Decentralized applications (dApps) are increasingly vulnerable to Sybil attacks, where malicious actors create multiple fake identities to manipulate the system. This can lead to various issues, including vote manipulation, resource exploitation, and unfair distribution of rewards.

## The Challenge of Sybil Resistance

Traditional solutions rely on centralized identity verification, which can compromise the decentralized nature of dApps. Moreover, relying on users to manually verify their identities can lead to low adoption rates and user fatigue.

## The Engineering Approach
