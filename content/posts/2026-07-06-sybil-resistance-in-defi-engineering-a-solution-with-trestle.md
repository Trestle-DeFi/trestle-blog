# Sybil Resistance in DeFi: Engineering a Solution with Trestle

## Problem Statement

Decentralized finance (DeFi) applications are increasingly vulnerable to sybil attacks, where malicious actors create multiple fake identities to manipulate market dynamics. This can lead to compromised security, unfair market outcomes, and a loss of user trust.

## The Engineering Challenge

Traditional identity verification methods, such as email or phone number-based authentication, are inadequate in the decentralized space due to the lack of a centralized authority. Moreover, relying solely on cryptographic techniques, like digital signatures, can be insufficient in preventing sybil attacks.

## Trestle's Solution

Trestle addresses the sybil resistance challenge by introducing a novel approach to decentralized identity verification. By leveraging a combination of cryptographic techniques, such as zero-knowledge proofs and homomorphic encryption, Trestle enables secure and scalable identity verification without relying on a centralized authority.
