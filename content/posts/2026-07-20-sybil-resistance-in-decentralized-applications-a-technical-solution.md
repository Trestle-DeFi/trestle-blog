# Sybil Resistance in Decentralized Applications: A Technical Solution

## Introduction

Decentralized applications (dApps) have become increasingly popular in recent years, but they are often plagued by a critical issue: Sybil resistance. In this article, we will explore the engineering approach to addressing this problem.

## The Problem of Sybil Resistance

Sybil resistance refers to the ability of a decentralized application to prevent an attacker from creating multiple fake identities or accounts (Sybils) to manipulate the system. This is a significant challenge in DeFi, as it can lead to various attacks, including vote manipulation and identity theft.

## Standard Solutions

*   CAPTCHAs: These are a common way to prevent bots from creating fake accounts, but they can be easily bypassed by sophisticated attackers.
