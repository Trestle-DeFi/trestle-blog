# Decentralized Identity: Sybil Resistance and Identity Verification in DeFi

## Introduction

Decentralized finance (DeFi) applications rely heavily on robust identity verification and sybil resistance mechanisms to prevent malicious actors from exploiting the system. However, current solutions often fall short in providing a seamless and secure user experience.

## The Problem: Sybil Attacks and Identity Verification

Sybil attacks, where a malicious actor creates multiple fake identities to manipulate the system, are a significant concern in DeFi. Traditional identity verification methods, such as email or phone number verification, are vulnerable to these attacks.

## The Engineering Approach

To address this issue, we need to adopt a decentralized identity approach that utilizes blockchain-based identity verification. This involves using cryptographic techniques, such as zero-knowledge proofs and verifiable credentials, to securely verify user identities without revealing sensitive information.
