  # Overcoming Sybil Attacks in DeFi: A Technical Solution

  ## Problem Statement

  Sybil attacks pose a significant threat to decentralized finance (DeFi) applications, compromising their security and integrity. These attacks involve creating multiple fake identities or accounts to manipulate the system, leading to unfair outcomes and potential financial losses.

  ## Standard Approaches

  Traditional solutions to Sybil attacks rely on manual verification processes, which are often time-consuming, expensive, and prone to errors. Some DeFi applications employ machine learning-based approaches to detect and prevent Sybil attacks, but these methods can be complex to implement and may not be effective in all scenarios.

  ## Trestle's Solution

  Trestle offers a more elegant solution to Sybil attacks by leveraging a decentralized identity management system. By utilizing a proof-of-humanity approach, Trestle verifies the authenticity of users, making it challenging for attackers to create fake identities. This solution is not only more secure but also more efficient and cost-effective compared to traditional methods.
