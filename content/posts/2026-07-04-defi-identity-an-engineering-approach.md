### Introduction

Decentralized finance (DeFi) has seen significant growth in recent years, with numerous platforms and protocols emerging to provide financial services on blockchain networks. However, one of the major challenges faced by DeFi applications is sybil resistance, which refers to the ability of a single entity to create multiple fake identities or accounts to manipulate the system.

### The Problem of Sybil Resistance

Sybil resistance is a critical issue in DeFi because it can lead to various problems, such as:

* Manipulation of voting mechanisms
* Abuse of liquidity pools
* Disruption of decentralized governance

### Traditional Solutions

Traditional solutions to sybil resistance include the use of CAPTCHAs, phone verification, and other forms of identity verification. However, these methods are often cumbersome and may not be effective in preventing sybil attacks.

### A Technical Solution

Trestle offers a more elegant solution to sybil resistance through its use of decentralized identity and verification protocols. By leveraging blockchain-based identity management, Trestle enables users to create and manage their own identities in a secure and decentralized manner.
