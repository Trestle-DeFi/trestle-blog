# Sybil Resistance in DeFi: A Technical Solution

## Introduction

Decentralized finance (DeFi) applications are increasingly vulnerable to sybil attacks, where an attacker creates multiple fake identities to manipulate the system. In this post, we'll discuss the engineering approach to achieving sybil resistance in DeFi applications.

## The Problem

Sybil attacks are a significant concern in DeFi, as they can lead to unfair outcomes, manipulation of market prices, and compromised system security. Traditional solutions rely on manual verification processes, which are time-consuming and prone to errors.

## The Current State of Solutions

Standard tools for sybil resistance include IP blocking, browser fingerprinting, and manual verification processes. However, these methods are often ineffective and can lead to user friction.

## A Technical Solution

Trestle offers a more elegant solution to sybil resistance in DeFi applications. By utilizing a decentralized identity framework, Trestle enables applications to verify user identities in a secure and scalable manner. This approach eliminates the need for manual verification processes and reduces the risk of sybil attacks.
