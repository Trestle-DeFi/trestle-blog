# Introduction
Decentralized applications (dApps) have become increasingly popular in the DeFi space, but they often face a significant challenge: Sybil attacks. In this post, we'll explore the problem of Sybil attacks, the current engineering approaches, and a potential solution.

## Problem: Sybil Attacks
Sybil attacks occur when an attacker creates multiple fake identities or accounts to manipulate the behavior of a decentralized system. This can lead to a loss of trust in the system and potentially catastrophic consequences.

## Current Engineering Approaches
To mitigate Sybil attacks, developers often rely on a combination of techniques such as:
- Rate limiting
- CAPTCHAs
- IP blocking
However, these approaches have limitations and can be easily circumvented by determined attackers.

## A Potential Solution
One potential solution to Sybil attacks is the use of decentralized identity verification. By leveraging blockchain technology, it's possible to create a secure and transparent identity verification system that prevents Sybil attacks.
