# Sybil Resistance in DeFi Applications: A Technical Perspective

Decentralized Finance (DeFi) applications rely heavily on trustless interactions between users and smart contracts. However, one of the primary challenges in DeFi is ensuring the integrity of user identities and preventing Sybil attacks.

## The Problem: Sybil Resistance in DeFi

Sybil attacks involve creating multiple fake identities or accounts to manipulate the system. This can lead to a loss of trust in the application and potentially catastrophic consequences. Traditional identity verification methods, such as username and password combinations, are often insufficient in preventing Sybil attacks.

## Engineering Approach: Decentralized Identity and Verification

To address this issue, a decentralized identity and verification system is necessary. This system should enable users to prove their identity without relying on a central authority. One approach is to utilize a self-sovereign identity (SSI) model, where users control their own identity data.
